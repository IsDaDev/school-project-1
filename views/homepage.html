About us

  • Who are we?

    • At Project MOSC, we are a dedicated team of automotive designers and racing enthusiasts united by our love for speed and innovation. Our diverse backgrounds in engineering, graphic design, and motorsport allow us to push the boundaries of racecar design.
    We believe that every racecar tells a unique story, and we aim to showcase this through captivating designs that represent the thrill of racing. As a close-knit community, we draw inspiration from the vibrant world of motorsport, inspiring professionals and enthusiasts alike with our creativity and craftsmanship.
    Join us on our journey as we celebrate the intersection of art and engineering, continually redefining what's possible in racecar design.

  • What are we trying to achieve?

    • Our mission is to educate and inspire. We share our passion for racecars with both enthusiasts and newcomers, fostering a deeper understanding of the artistry, engineering, and history behind these incredible machines.
    Through engaging content and stunning presentations, we highlight the unique features and innovations of each racecar. We also showcase our designs, inviting our audience to appreciate the beauty and performance of these vehicles.
    Together, we aim to create a community where knowledge and passion intersect, celebrating the rich heritage of racecars while paving the way for future innovations in design and performance.
